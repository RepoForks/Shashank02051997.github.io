MyLibretto

iOS & Android App

MyLibretto is an application created for any university student who wants to keep track of his university career. With MyLibretto you can see in a moment your weighted/arithmetic average and the number of ECTS earned, moreover you can get a report on your academic performance and also see how it can change in relation to the result of your next exam. MyLibretto was created with the aim of being used by any university student. For this reason, you can enter the different parameters required for the correct computation of your average, considering all your university rules. The application includes the complete list of all Italian universities to choose from.
