MyTime

iOS App

The best events in London in your pocket. Keeping up with everything that’s going on in London is tough. Whatever you’re into, be it food, music, free stuff, the arts there’s always something for everyone. MyTime provides a personalised events calendar for London. You tell the app what you’re interested in, from the mainstream to the uber trendy off-the-grid stuff and it will tell you what’s happening on a specific date. The app quickly became one of the top apps for Londoners, serving the top picks from the event industry.
