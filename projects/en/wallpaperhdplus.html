Wallpaper HD +

Android App

Wallpaper HD + is a wallpaper app powered by Wallpaper Abyss, one of the most important and famous wallpaper provider. A collection of more than 500.000 HD wallpapers available right now on your device. You can save your favorite wallpapers, search by categories and key words, and set up a wallpaper album in order to change automatically your wallpaper after a fixed amount of time
